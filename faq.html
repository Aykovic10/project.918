How to order

Project.918 uses Buy Me a Coffee for the final payment step. The website creates your order details first, then you paste them into the BMC message box when paying.

01. Choose your item

Open the product, select your size, color, and quantity, then add it to your bag.

02. Fill shipping details

In the bag, add your name, email, country, and full shipping address.

03. Checkout with BMC

Click checkout. Your order details are copied automatically. In Buy Me a Coffee, enter the exact total and paste the copied order details into the message box.

04. We process the order

Once payment is confirmed, your order is prepared and shipped. Packages are on their way within 10 business days.
